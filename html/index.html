Skyfall Silks offers circus experiences for adults and kids of all ages, including workshops, lessons and performances. Our vision is to give everyone a chance to have a go in a fun and uncomplicated environment, under safe supervision.

↓ Learn more ↓



Workshops

Skyfall Silks offers the ultimate interactive circus experience in form of aerial workshops (silks, trapeze, Lyra) for public events, such as festivals or shows, or private parties. For people waiting their turn, there's always hula hoops, juggling gear ect available to play with.

Lessons

We offer weekly aerial lessons, mainly on aerial silks and static trapeze. We warm up in a fun way with either hula hoops, staffs, juggling balls or pois. Our classes are small, up to 6 people only, parent/kids teams welcome. The classes are held on an 8m outdoor rig, with all safety assured, including a large crash mat.In case of rain, we run the classes in a smaller area undercover.

Performances

We offer aerial performances, specially as part of an evening show, including lights and special effects.
