Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

How much space so you need to set up the portable rig?

The rig is 7.5x7.5x7.5m in a triangle, but I need some space to set up, generally 9 square metres of flat ground is sufficient.

Have you got a smaller set up?

Yes, we can set up a 4.5m tripod, needing 6m square. It's also only 4.5m high. That makes it easier to fit undercover at time too.

Do you have insurance?

Yes, with your quote we supply you with a copy of our insurance.

What safely equipment do you use?

We have professional thick crash mats from HARTS.

How many kids are in a class?

Normally between 2 and 4, unless there are mir siblings. We generally have 2 apparatuses up at the one time.

Can I join a class with my kids?

Yes, we have a few mums doing classes with their kids

Do you warm up before lessons?

Yes, we normally warm our muscles up with hula hoops, learning a skill at the same time.

What do I need to bring /wear?

Long thighs would be ideal, or long stretchy pants, and a sports top. Please bring a water bottle.

Do I need any previous experience? Or gymnastic background?

No, no experience necessary at all, any level can do something. You'll get experience and strength as you go, and surprise yourself what you can do, in a short amount of time.

What's the youngest age you teach?

I've had 2 year old siblings, they normally just join their siblings for a short while. Some 4-5 year olds enjoy it, but generally 6 is a good age.

Do you work towards competitions?

Not really, we sometimes work towards a show, we host here, but mainly do it for fun and fitness.
